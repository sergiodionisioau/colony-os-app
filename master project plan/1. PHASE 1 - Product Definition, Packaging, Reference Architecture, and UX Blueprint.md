3.4 Product entities that must be defined now

Commercial customer account.

A business operating instance under a tenant.

Environment

A runtime boundary:

dev
staging
prod
edge
Module

A packaged business or technical capability that can be attached/detached/upgraded.

Template

A preconfigured workspace composition.

Connector

An integration endpoint to third-party systems or data sources.

Orchestrator

The selected agent/workflow coordination engine.

Desired State

What the customer wants enabled.

Current State

What is actually installed and running.

Change Plan

The deterministic delta between desired and current state.

Checkpoint

Rollback-safe snapshot marker.
