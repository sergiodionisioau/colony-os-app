21. 3 critical edge cases
Edge case 1 — Downgrade while over limits

A tenant attempts to downgrade from Business to Pro while currently exceeding:

allowed seats
active premium modules
advanced orchestrator entitlement
data retention or workspace feature limits

Required behavior:

show exact incompatibilities
block immediate destructive downgrade where necessary
offer resolution path
show future-effective downgrade rules clearly
do not silently disable critical features mid-flow
Edge case 2 — Past-due during active onboarding

A new customer starts onboarding from a paid trial or converted subscription, but billing becomes past due before onboarding completes.

Required behavior:

show billing issue clearly without corrupting onboarding state
preserve workspace and onboarding progress according to policy
explain which actions are limited and which remain accessible
provide payment recovery path
avoid contradictory “success” and “blocked” messaging
Edge case 3 — Cancellation during active operations

A tenant initiates cancellation while:

exports are in progress
template upgrade is in progress
long-running workflows are active
agents are still executing

Required behavior:

show affected operations
require explicit cancellation timing choice if policy supports it
preserve safe completion or controlled interruption rules
expose export/recovery implications
record evidence for cancellation and resulting operational state

27. Acceptance test for Phase 9

Ask these exact questions.

Discovery test

Can a first-time visitor understand what the product is, what the plans are, and what to do next without ambiguity?

Purchase test

Can a buyer complete checkout with a clear summary and land in the correct post-purchase state?

Entitlement test

Can a user always tell what is included, what is locked, why it is locked, and how to unlock it?

Billing test

Can an admin understand invoices, renewal, payment state, seats, and subscription status without confusion?

Cancellation test

Can a customer cancel or downgrade with consequence visibility, export awareness, and no dark patterns?

Support test

Can a user reach help, release notes, and support from any critical blocked or confusing state?

Trust test

Can the product prove that pricing, entitlements, billing, and commercial state changes are consistent and auditable?

If any answer is no, Phase 9 is not done.
